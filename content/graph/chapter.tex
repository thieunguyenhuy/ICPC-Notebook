\section{Graph Theory}
	\subsection{Kirchhoff's Theorem}
		% Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kirchhoff%27s_theorem
		% Test: stress-tests/graph/kirchhoff.cpp
		The number of spanning trees in a graph is equal to any cofactor of its Laplacian matrix $L$. $L$ is defined as $D - A$, where $D$ is the degree matrix (a diagonal matrix with vertex degrees on the diagonal) and $A$ is the adjacency matrix.
